About Hartford India Insurance

Hartford India Insurance has been serving customers with trust and transparency for over 25 years. Our mission is to protect what matters most to you. For more than 200 years, we have provided people and businesses with the support and protection they need to pursue their unique ambitions, seize opportunity and prevail through unexpected challenges. We view underwriting human achievement to be at the heart of what we do, aiming to build resilience in our communities, empower personal and business success for our customers and create a culture where our employees can thrive. Our leadership team is steadfast in its commitment to perform transparently, to serve as an inspiration to others and above all to enable human achievement. We dare to explore new opportunities and move quickly for our company and customers. Acting with integrity and winning the right way matters above all. The Hartford’s deep commitment to sustainability is rooted in our proud legacy and lives at the core of our long-term business strategy. We understand what it takes to be sustainable and we’re doing it – actively listening and adapting so we can remain relevant to our customers and distribution partners, all while operating in a way that instills trust and confidence. We are committed to sustaining our exceptional culture. It sets us apart, drives business value, creates a sense of belonging for employees and is what makes The Hartford, The Hartford.
